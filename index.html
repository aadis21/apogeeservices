Skill Development Training (DDUGKY Project)

At apogee Services, we’re not only passionate about serving businesses—we’re equally committed to building a more skilled and employable India. As part of our social impact initiatives, we are actively engaged in the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDUGKY) in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

Our DDUGKY project focuses on empowering rural youth through industry-aligned skill development programs. With a learner-centric approach and certified trainers, we’ve successfully trained over 600 candidates so far. Our curriculum blends practical, theoretical, and soft skills components, ensuring that each candidate is job-ready and confident upon completion.

Placement is not just a goal—it’s a promise. We are proud to have facilitated employment for more than 400 candidates across reputable organizations and growing industries. And we’re just getting started. Our team is continuously expanding outreach and strengthening partnerships to train and place even more candidates, transforming individual lives and uplifting entire communities.

This initiative is aligned with apogee Services’ broader purpose: driving sustainable development through capability building. It’s our way of bridging the gap between rural potential and urban opportunity—ensuring that every individual, regardless of background, has the skills and support needed to thrive in today’s economy.

We invite stakeholders, employers, and community partners to join us in scaling this mission and delivering lasting impact.
